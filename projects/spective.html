SPECTIVE COFFEE BRANDING

Product Design

51

Overview

Coffee has been the cornerstone during my tenure at School of the Art Institute. It would only make sense that tackling product design for a coffee brand. Traveling to different cafes around the city with my partner inspired me to take up the challenge of doing branding surrounding my fake coffee brand "SPECTIVE COFFEE"

SPECTIVE is a play on PERSPECTIVE. Bringing a new angle to your everyday coffeee. All roasted in house, our team of specially trained baristas will offer you a drink you'll always come back for.

The design idea behind SPECTIVE is simple, and wanting to let customers know that there are no bells and whistles attached. I also considered the environments that coffee bags live in most.

Although you could pick this up at grocery stores, places like office spaces, teacher's lounges, hotel lobbies are places you often see loose bags of coffee. The design of the bag acts as a piece of modern art, bringing a pop of color to any environment it sits in.
