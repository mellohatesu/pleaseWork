OVERVIEW

This is a trailer made for a music project featuring 20+ artists. My creative collective, Meowballz Online, was approached to make a trailer. I contributed to a cut as a supervisor as well and composed the entire blend of the music featured within the project.

The project is slated to come out Q4 of 2024 or Q1 of 2025. My music will be released along side of notable artists such as Gum.mp3, Dazegxd, and TsubiClub.

A challenge me and my team came across while working on this project was making multiple genres of music visably cohesive. Despite this, using the central theme of the project and piecing together different vibes from the source material led us to success.

Fooly Cooly is one of my favorite anime and I am extremely happy with the end product of this trailer as well as the upcoming music.
