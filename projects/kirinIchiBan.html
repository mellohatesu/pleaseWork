Overview

Magazine advertisment for beer, Kirin Ichiban, showcasing the elegance and versitlilty of the beverge. I took up the subject of this project after having a glass at an Izakaya, the taste was unlike anything I've had before.

As I worked on this project, I made sure to highlight that this drink can be enjoyed in casual and in professional settings.

This project helped me understand the fundamentals of type, as well as how to build a spread.
