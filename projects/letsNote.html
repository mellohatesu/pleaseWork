LET'S NOTE SPREAD (2023)

Visual Communication

514



Overview

Mock informational spread for "Let's Note", a line of compact laptops out of Japan. I ended receving one of these devices as a gift earlier this year, and fell in love with it. I am a big fan of compact and utilitarian technology, so making this spread almost felt like a natural thank you to this device.

I honestly wish that the United States manufactured devices this sleek and made for work. It would definitely help me cut down on scrolling

After building in inDesign, I took it to After Effects to give it some motion.

This project was great for me to hone and clean up my After Effects skills, later working on the Goshi Project. A lot of the principles and techniques I picked up here bled over into future projects.

This project helped me understand the fundamentals of type, as well as how to build a spread.
