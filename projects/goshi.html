GOSHI SHORT-FORM MOTION BUMP (2024)

Motion Graphics

1324


Bringing brand identity to life through motion.

For this 30-second animated bumper, I collaborated with Goshi, a Japanese-American self-care brand, to craft a visually compelling and dynamic ad. From initial concept to final execution, I led storyboarding, composition, and post-production, ensuring a seamless blend of design and movement.

All visual assets were created by my frequent collaborator Ricky Pacas, with whom I co-founded Studio2Tek. Beyond animation, I also composed the original score in FL Studio, handling all mixing and mastering to create an immersive audiovisual experience.

Having hands-on experience in the field is invaluable, but working with a brand of this scale was an entirely new challenge—one that pushed me to refine my skills and elevate my approach to design and storytelling. It was a learning experience that made me a stronger, more adaptable creative.

The result? A polished, brand-aligned motion piece that enhances Goshi’s identity and elevates its market presence. Since launch, the bumper has gained strong audience engagement, amassing nearly 1,000 likes within the first month—and counting.
