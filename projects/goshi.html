GOSHI SHORT-FORM MOTION BUMP (2024)

Motion Graphics

1324

Overview

30-second bumper for Japanese/American self-care company, Goshi. I was responsible for storyboarding, composition, and post-production of this project. All assets were created by frequent collaborator, Ricky Pacas, with whom I co-founded Studio2Tek.

I also composed the original score in FL Studio, with mixing and mastering done by me. Despite the extensive work (a 3-month project), I would take on a project like this again in a heartbeat.

As of now, this advertisement has generated nearly 1,000 likes within its first month and is climbing every day.

This project allowed me the creative freedom to integrate all the mediums I've explored previously, weaving together my skills in music production, storyboarding, and graphic design into a cohesive product. Working with a client of this caliber was truly exciting and taught me a lot about staying in close communication with clients.

Managing schedules, setting up meetings, keeping the team on track, and creating presentations ensured that the team and the client were always aligned.
