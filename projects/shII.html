SEVENTH HEAVEN II (2024)

Production Manager

124





OVERVIEW

Promotion matieral for "Seventh Heaven II" a Chicago Jungle Rave. Similar to the original, this project involved crafting a trailer for an upcoming DJ set, this time drawing inspiration from the visual aesthetics of JRPGs (Japanese Role Playing Games).

While I remixed a Persona song for it, I oversaw the live event, as well as all of the social media promotion for the project. All assets made by collaborator, Ricky Pacas. This project helped me learn a lot of fundamentals of visual marketing, the principles of typography, and how to work on a project of this size.

Working on this project was extremely fun from a design standpoint. Finding the intersection between rave events and video games is something that truly resonated with my character. I believe this project shows my passion for anything that I put my pen to.

I was able to learn what goes into making a flashy flyer and market to the dancers of Chicago. I gained a lot of insight into the Chicago music scene and was able to connect with artits for future work.
